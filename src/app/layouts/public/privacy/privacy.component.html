Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2017

Since the nature of our business is protecting your privacy, we feel that you should fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the use of that information.

We provide Virtual Private Networking (VPN) services and other Internet privacy features. This Privacy Policy describes what information we keep and how we use it.

Disclosure

Operating under the jurisdiction of Iceland allows us to guarantee to you our no-logging policy. This means your online activities through our privacy service are not monitored, recorded, logged, stored or passed to any third party.

User Data

1. When you visit our website:

If you make inquiries through our website, you can provide your name and email address. This information will only be used to respond to your inquiries and requests. The information is not used for any other purpose.

If you choose to share our website or services with someone by clicking "share this with a friend,” you might be providing your referral ID, email address, social media profiles, or other information, as well as that of the recipient. This information is used only to refer our services to the recipient, and reward you for participating in any affiliate or referral incentive programs. The information is not used for any other purpose.

Cookies

We utilize cookies in various parts of our website and software, such as signing in, billing and subscription management, and other functionality. We also integrate with various third party services, which may use cookies to improve your experience. This includes but is not limited to Google Analytics for the statistical assessment and the website performance improvement, Zendesk for customer support, SnapEngage for customer live chat.

However, you can opt-out of accepting these cookies by disabling cookies in your browser. If you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You don’t need to have cookies enabled to view our website, but you do need cookies to purchase and use our services, submit any support requests, or utilize other important functionality of our website and services.

We use affiliate and referral identifiers to reward the affiliates and referrers of our website and services with incentives and other revenue sharing programs.

Customer Service

We keep our live chat and e-mail conversations with you, unless you request this information to be deleted.



2. When you sign up for our service:

In order to provide our users with quality customer service, we keep a few pieces of information:

- E-mail address. We ask for your e-mail address as part of your account registration. This ensures that we can communicate with you when we have issues regarding your account and send you notifications regarding updates to our services.

- Payment data. Although we encourage our users to purchase our service with Bitcoin for the maximum level of privacy, we also offer alternative payment methods for your convenience. We do not store your name, any credit card numbers, or address information. We only keep the relevant transaction data needed for refund requests and to provide you with customer service.



3. While you use our software:

When using our Cypherpunk Privacy service, your Internet traffic will be encrypted according to your selected cryptographic profile and anonymized by mixing it with many other users’ traffic. Using strong encryption to connect to our privacy service will make your online activities opaque to governments, ISPs, and third party adversaries.

Furthermore, we have a strict no-logging policy when it comes to user activity online: being based in Iceland, a country which offers the most Internet privacy and freedom in the world, we are not required to perform any data storage or reporting of any kind, and we are empowered to deny any third-party requests.



4. While you are connected to our network:

Our backend infrastructure constantly load balances our users across a network of servers to continuously optimize the speed and performance of our service. This ensures that we can provide you with a high level of availability, with consistently fast speeds throughout our entire privacy protection network. Our backend infrastructure does not observe any individual user traffic, and is only for these network optimization purposes.

If, and only if a user opts-in, our software may send diagnostic and analytics data for the purpose of reporting connection errors, possible bugs in our application, and optimizing the user experience. The information does not contain personally identifying information.



In summary, we only know enough to contact you for customer service and to enhance your experience using our service. None of your Internet traffic or personal data is logged or collected.



Policy Changes

You understand that our present Privacy Policy is subject to changes made by us, at any time, in our sole discretion. You understand that it is your obligation to periodically review our Privacy Policy in order to account for any changes made.

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we keep, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
