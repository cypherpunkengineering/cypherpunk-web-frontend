When using our Services, you agree to let us administer your data as we describe in this Privacy Policy.

Cypherpunk is a global company, with headquarters in Japan. Although our physical servers are located in many different countries around the world, Cypherpunk does not store personally identifiable information outside of Japan's physical borders. By using our services, you authorize Cypherpunk to use your information according to Japan's laws, regardless of which country you are located in.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy (at) cypherpunk.com

2.2 Disclosure of Information to Third Parties

Except as described below, Cypherpunk Privacy will NOT disclose any information to other commercial parties under any circumstance.

In the event Cypherpunk Privacy is required to comply with law enforcement where subpoenas, warrants or other legal documents have been provided, valid under Canadian jurisdiction, the extent of disclosure is limited to the “Personal Information” listed within this privacy policy.

As noted above, Cypherpunk Privacy utilizes PCI-compliant third party payment processors to collect your credit card and other billing information.

If our organization structure changes (i.e we undergo a restructuring or are acquired), we may need to migrate your Personal Information to a third party related to a business transaction, but, we will ensure that such a third party has entered into an agreement under which the use of your Personal Information is only related to purposes necessary for the transaction.

Cypherpunk Privacy does NOT store users originating IP addresses when connected to our service and thus cannot identify users when provided IP addresses of our servers. Additionally, we cannot disclose information about the applications, services or websites our users consume while connected to our Services; as Cypherpunk Privacy does NOT store this information.



2.3 Consent

Unless otherwise required by law, we will obtain your consent whenever we collect your Personal Information or make changes to the Operational Information we store. Your consent may be expressed or implied. In certain circumstances your consent may be implied by your actions. For example, by providing us Personal Information to sign up for our Service, it is implied that we can use such information as we outlined in this privacy policy.

The form of consent sought by Cypherpunk Privacy may vary depending on the nature of the information. In determining the appropriate form of consent, Cypherpunk Privacy will take into account the sensitivity of the information and your reasonable expectations. Implied consent will generally be appropriate where information is less sensitive. You have the right to withhold your consent on any request to use your Personal Information. To exercise your choices, or ask questions about your Personal Information, please contact: privacy (at) cypherpunk.com



2.4 Limiting Collection

We take great care to not collect Personal Information indiscriminately and limit collection to the minimum necessary information required to operate our service. By limiting the collection of data, we help to protect the privacy and security of your Personal Information.



2.5 Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention

We will not use your Personal Information for any purpose that you have not consented to. Cypherpunk Privacy will NOT sell or trade Personal Information for commercial purposes.

Only Cypherpunk Privacy’s employees with a business need to know or whose duties require, are granted access to our customers’ Personal Information. All such employees will be required as a condition of employment to respect the confidentiality of our customers’ Personal Information.

We store your Personal Information only as long as it is necessary for the purposes for which it is collected. We erase or destroy the records containing Personal Information when they are no longer required; this will be done in ways that will ensure your continued privacy.
